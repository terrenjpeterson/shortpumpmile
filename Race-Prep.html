Packet Pickup

Packet Pickup will be held in front of American Family Fitness, Short Pump Mall location, Richmond VA.

What is a packet pickup?

This is where you will get your race number to wear on the front of your shirt on race day, where you will get your Short Pump Race T-Shirt, and other give-aways that we have to hand out from our various sponsors. This is also where you can ask any last minute questions that haven't been covered on this web page.

At this time we will also take any last minute registrations if race is still open. Please visit the Short Pump Race website for any up to the minute changes.

Packet Pickup Dates and Times:
Friday, September 28th, 3-7pm
Saturday, September 29th, 10am-5pm

You need to make every effort to retrieve your race packets at this time. You may have a friend or teacher pick up your child's packet, but please make sure that you know that they are doing this for you to avoid confusion.

Limited options will exist at packet pickup, so register today!
